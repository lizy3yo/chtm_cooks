Custodian Responsibilities

  • Inventory Management: Add, update, archive, and maintain all inventory items
  • Request Processing: Review, approve, or reject student requests for items
  • Donation Management: Record and process donated items into the inventory system
  • Item Inspection: Conduct quality checks, condition assessments, and damage inspections
  • Stock Control: Monitor stock levels, track item usage, and manage replacements
  • Report Generation: Create inventory reports, usage statistics, and operational summaries
  • Operational Authority: Full CRUD operations on inventory and request-related data

Key Processes

  • 1.0 Authentication & Authorization: Validates custodian credentials and grants operational access
  • 3.0 Manage Inventory: Full control over inventory items including add, update, archive operations
  • 4.0 Process Requests: Reviews and processes student requests, updates request status
  • 5.0 Manage Donations: Records donated items and integrates them into inventory
  • 6.0 Item Inspection: Conducts inspections, records condition, and updates item status
  • 7.0 Generate Reports: Creates operational reports for inventory and request management
